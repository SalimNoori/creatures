Creatures

A JavaScript data attributes example by Salim Noori

This page demonstrates how to use JavaScript data attributes to differentiate data.

When you click Show Creature Home, the background color will change based on their natural environment:

  • land: green
  • water: blue
  • air: yellow

When you click Show Creature Type, the border color and style will change based on the type of creature:

  • animal: solid red
  • insect: dashed orange
